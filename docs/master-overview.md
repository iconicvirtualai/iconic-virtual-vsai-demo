# Overview

Iconic Virtual AI is a nationwide AI-powered virtual staging platform designed for real estate professionals, photographers, marketers, and property owners.

Users can:
- Upload property photos
- Generate AI virtual staging renders
- Purchase credits
- Subscribe to plans
- Download completed images
- Manage render history and billing

The platform supports:
- DIY AI staging
- Traditional designer staging
- Subscription billing
- Watermarked previews
- Automated rendering workflows
