Frequently Asked Questions

What is virtual staging?+
Virtual staging digitally furnishes empty rooms in your listing photos, letting buyers see a home's potential without the cost or hassle of physical staging.
How long does it take?+
Most images are ready in 30–60 seconds. Bulk jobs of 10+ images complete within 5 minutes.
Is virtual staging MLS-compliant?+
Yes, in most MLSs — provided you disclose that the image is virtually staged. We include a small "Virtually Staged" badge option for compliance.
Do credits expire?+
One-time credit packs never expire. Monthly subscription credits roll over for 30 days.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What file formats are supported?+
We accept JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WebP up to 50MB per file. Output is delivered in JPG and PNG at up to 4K resolution.

Popular Help Articles

How do I improve staging quality?+
Use well-lit, high-resolution photos taken from a corner angle. Avoid cluttered rooms or poor lighting. Higher source quality = better results.
My staging failed — what now?+
Failed jobs are automatically refunded. Click "Retry" on the order page or contact support if it happens repeatedly.
Can I get a refund?+
We offer full refunds on unused credits within 30 days. Contact support to request a refund.
How do I download in bulk?+
Open any project page and click "Download All" to get a ZIP of every staged image.
