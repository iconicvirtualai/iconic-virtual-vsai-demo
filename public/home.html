FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

How quickly can I get my staged photos?+
AI staged images are ready instantly (under 60 seconds). Traditional staging by our design team is delivered within 24 hours of order confirmation.
What's the difference between AI and traditional staging?+
AI Virtual Staging uses AI to stage photos instantly from design references. Traditional staging uses our human design team to manually customize each image for precise, magazine-quality results.
Can I try before I buy?+
Yes! Upload any room photo and see it staged instantly with our AI tool. No credit card, no sign-up. If you like the result, purchase the high-res version. No obligation.
Do you offer revisions?+
Yes. AI images include free revision options, and traditional staging includes 1-2 free redesigns per image depending on your plan.
What file formats do you accept?+
We accept JPG and PNG files. For best results, use high-resolution photos taken with good lighting and wide-angle shots when possible.
Is virtual staging MLS-compliant?+
Yes, in most MLSs as long as you disclose that images are virtually staged. We include a "Virtually Staged" badge option for compliance.
Can I cancel my membership anytime?+
Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks. You keep access through the end of your billing period, and unused credits roll over for 30 days.
